# Loqa Community Code of Conduct

## 🌟 Our Commitment

The Loqa community is dedicated to providing a **welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free experience** for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We are committed to building technology that respects user autonomy and privacy, and we extend that same respect to every member of our community.

---

## 🎯 Our Values

### 🤝 Collaboration Over Competition
- We build together, not against each other
- Success is measured by community growth and collective achievement
- We share knowledge and help others succeed

### 🌱 Growth Through Learning
- Everyone was a beginner once - we support learning journeys
- Mistakes are opportunities for growth and improvement
- We provide constructive feedback and mentorship

### 🔒 Privacy & Respect
- Just as Loqa respects user privacy, we respect individual boundaries
- Personal information and private communications are protected
- Consent is required for sharing personal details or experiences

### 🌍 Diversity & Inclusion
- We welcome contributors from all backgrounds and experience levels
- Different perspectives make our project stronger
- We actively work to remove barriers to participation

### 🛠️ Innovation Through Community
- The best ideas come from diverse voices and experiences
- We encourage experimentation and creative problem-solving
- Community needs drive our development priorities

---

## ✅ Expected Behavior

### In All Interactions
- **Be respectful and considerate** in all communications
- **Use welcoming and inclusive language** that makes others feel valued
- **Be patient and helpful** with questions and learning processes
- **Focus on what's best for the community** and the project
- **Show empathy** towards other community members

### In Technical Discussions
- **Provide constructive feedback** that helps improve contributions
- **Be specific and actionable** in suggestions and critiques
- **Acknowledge good work** and celebrate contributions
- **Assume good intentions** and ask for clarification when needed
- **Focus on ideas and code**, not individuals

### In Community Spaces
- **Be inclusive** in conversations and decision-making
- **Help newcomers** get oriented and feel welcome
- **Share knowledge freely** and support learning
- **Respect different levels of experience** and technical knowledge
- **Maintain confidentiality** when requested

---

## ❌ Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not tolerated in our community:

### Harassment & Discrimination
- Offensive comments related to gender, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, body size, race, ethnicity, religion, or nationality
- Deliberate intimidation, stalking, or following
- Unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Sustained disruption of discussions or events

### Disrespectful Communication
- Personal attacks, insults, or derogatory language
- Trolling, deliberately inflammatory comments, or bad-faith arguments
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information without permission

### Technical Misconduct
- Submitting malicious code or intentionally harmful contributions
- Violating intellectual property rights
- Circumventing security measures or access controls
- Spamming issues, PRs, or discussions with irrelevant content

### Community Disruption
- Repeatedly violating community guidelines after warnings
- Impersonating other community members or project maintainers
- Promoting competing projects in inappropriate contexts
- Organizing brigading or coordinated disruption

## 🛡️ Reporting & Enforcement

### How to Report
If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior:

1. **For immediate safety concerns**: Contact local emergency services
2. **For harassment or serious violations**: Email conduct@loqalabs.com
3. **For minor issues**: Reach out to community moderators on Discord
4. **For public discussions**: Use GitHub's reporting features

### What to Include
When reporting, please provide:
- **Description** of the incident and behavior
- **Context** (where and when it occurred)
- **Impact** (how it affected you or others)
- **Evidence** (screenshots, links, etc.) if available
- **Preferred resolution** (if you have one in mind)

### Response Process
Our community team will:

1. **Acknowledge** your report within 24 hours
2. **Investigate** thoroughly and fairly
3. **Protect** the privacy of all involved parties
4. **Take action** as appropriate
5. **Follow up** to ensure resolution

### Possible Actions
Depending on the severity and context, responses may include:

- **Warning** with guidance on expected behavior
- **Temporary suspension** from community spaces
- **Permanent ban** from the project and community
- **Escalation** to law enforcement if necessary
