### ❤️ About Loqa

**Loqa is not just a voice assistant — it’s a platform for building respectful, local-first voice experiences.**

Created by a transgender engineer and open-source advocate, Loqa exists because most smart assistants aren’t built for people like us — they’re built for surveillance, scale, and profit.

Loqa is different:
- It runs locally, not in the cloud  
- It’s built as a modular, open platform — not a locked-down product  
- It prioritizes consent, transparency, and trust at every layer  

We explicitly invite marginalized creators — trans folks, disabled devs, neurodivergent thinkers, and people of color — to help shape the future of private, user-owned voice interfaces.

**Your sponsorship helps us sustain this vision — and build a better, safer voice ecosystem for everyone.**
